Going For Gold Fitness Tracker

Turn your daily activity into a fun, competitive fitness journey using your Apple Health data.

What it does:

  • Converts your steps and workouts into points
  • Set your own monthly targets to earn bronze, silver, and gold medals
  • Compete with friends in private leagues

Key Features:

  • Works with Apple Health (steps and workouts from any device that syncs to Apple Health)
  • Age-adjusted workout intensity targets
  • Privacy-focused: only points are shared, never your health data
  • Track progress month by month

Getting Started

  1. Grant Apple Health access
  2. Set your medal targets
  3. Set apple watch on/off and set your age to track workouts (in Settings)
  4. Optional: Create a cloud account to join leagues
  5. View your progress in the app

How Points Work

Daily Steps:

  • 7k steps = 3 points
  • 10k steps = 5 points
  • 12.5k+ steps = 8 points

Workouts (30+ minutes):

Points based on heart rate intensity relative to your age, see Advanced notes below.

Default targets: 120 points (bronze), 140 (silver), 160 (gold) - fully customizable

Advanced Notes

Workout Points Calculation

For workouts with at least 30 minutes duration, points are awarded based on average heart rate:

  • 5 points: Average heart rate ≥ (220 - user age) × 0.6
  • 8 points: Average heart rate ≥ (220 - user age) × 0.7

Examples:

For age 49:

  • Max HR: 171 bpm (220 - 49)
  • 5 points: 103+ bpm (60% of 171)
  • 8 points: 120+ bpm (70% of 171)

For age 20:

  • Max HR: 200 bpm (220 - 20)
  • 5 points: 120+ bpm
  • 8 points: 140+ bpm

User age can be set in Settings.
