What it does:

  • Converts your steps and workouts into points
  • Set your own monthly targets to earn bronze, silver, and gold medals- no prizes/awards involved just motivation to do more!
  • Compete with friends in private leagues

Key Features:

  • Works with Apple Health (steps and workouts from any device that syncs to Apple Health)
  • Age-adjusted workout intensity targets
  • Privacy-focused: only points are shared, never your health data
  • Track progress month by month
